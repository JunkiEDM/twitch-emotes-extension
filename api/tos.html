Terms of Service of Emotes Showcase

These are the Terms of Service for the Emotes Showcase Twitch Extension.

  • By enabling this extension on your channel, you agree that your sub emote art is displayed in the emote panel. You can choose to hide groups of emotes. The extension does not re-host the emotes on its own services.
  • Support is on an as-possible basis, there are no guaranteed issue resolution timeframes that I can offer.
  • This extension relies on various third parties to provide the emote information for a channel. Those third parties may change or cease their offering at any time, leading to the extension no longer finding or displaying emotes.
  • The extension is primarily hosted on infrastructure provided by Twitch. Service of that infrastructure is out of the extension author's hands.
  • Some of the data is cached by a service provided by the extension author. Operations of that service are on a best-effort basis as long as the extension is officially available for Twitch.
  • Older versions of the extension may not work properly. The extension should be auto updated as long as it is normally installed through the provided methods on Twitch.
